# Privacy Policy — TackMath

_Last updated: 2026-06-21_

TackMath is a sailing tack-angle calculator for iPhone. Your privacy is simple to
describe because **TackMath does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

## What the app accesses

- **Location (compass heading & GPS course).** TackMath uses your device's compass
  heading and GPS course-over-ground solely to display your current bow direction,
  heel, speed, and computed tack angles **in real time, on your device.** This happens
  only while the app is open and you have granted "While Using the App" location access.

## What we do with it

- **Nothing leaves your device.** Heading, course, speed, and motion data are read from
  the device sensors, used to render the on-screen compass and readouts, and then
  discarded. They are never recorded to a file, sent over the network, or shared with
  the developer or any third party.
- **No analytics, no tracking, no accounts.** TackMath contains no analytics SDKs, no
  advertising, no third-party tracking, and no user accounts or logins.
- **No data collection.** Because no data is collected, there is nothing to sell,
  retain, export, or delete.

## Permissions

TackMath requests one permission: **Location (While Using the App)**. You can grant or
revoke it at any time in **Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → TackMath**.
Denying location access means the compass and tack-angle readouts cannot function, but no
other part of the app is affected.

## Children's privacy

TackMath collects no data from anyone, including children.
